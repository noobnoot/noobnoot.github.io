Welcome!

I made this STEM library to provide a simple web-based resources for my students in the field of STEM. Coding has been my leisure and passion, and I want to integrate this skill into my teaching.

This website (hosted through GitHub Pages) is run by vanilla JavaScript (ES6) and JQuery through custom HTML components. The source code can be accessed through my GitHub repository.

My favorite STEM subjects would be Mathematics, Biology, and Chemistry (hence this library will focus on these). Above is Evogeneao Tree of Life diagram just for a reference. An interactive version is available here.

The next section is a structure of all subtopics in this STEM library.

General Biology 1

[GENBIO1] [BIOLOGY I] [GRADE 11/12]

Subject Description: This subject is designed to enhance the understanding of the principles and concepts in the study of biology, particulary life processes at the cellular and molecular levels.

General Biology

This section will cover the basic principles of biology and the fundamental concepts of life processes.

1. Introduction to Biology

2. Basic Cell Biology

3. Cell Cycle and Cell Division

Biological Molecules

This section will cover the fundamental biological molecules and their roles in living systems.

1. Carbohydrates

2. Lipids

3. Proteins

3. Nucleic Acids

Physical Science

[PHYSCI] [PHYSICAL SCIENCE] [GRADE 11/12]

Subject Description: Evolution of our understanding of matter, motion, electricity, magnetism, light, and the universe from ancient times to the present.
